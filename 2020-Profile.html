Company Name Saudi Arabia Fertilizers Co (2020)
Company Purpose Saudi Arabia Fertilizers Co (known as: SAFCO) is a public company, listed on Tadawul since January 1993. SAFCO operates within the materials sector focusing on materials. It has Companies operating across Saudi Arabia. SAFCO is based in Jubail, Saudi Arabia and was established in September 1965.
